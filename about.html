// faqs

Got Questions?

Is QuizMaster completely free?

Yes! QuizMaster is 100% free with no hidden charges, subscriptions, or paywalls.

How does the timer work?

Each question has a 20-second countdown. If time runs out, the question is marked as skipped and the correct answer is revealed. A total timer also tracks your full quiz time.

How many categories are there?

Currently 6 categories — Science, History, Geography, Technology, Sports, and Pop Culture — each with 10 questions and its own dedicated quiz.

How is the leaderboard score calculated?

Scores are based on correct answers, quiz difficulty, and speed. Completing streaks gives bonus multipliers.

Is there a mobile app?

Not yet — but our website is fully responsive and works great on mobile browsers. A dedicated app is on our roadmap for 2026.
